Frequently asked Questions

  1. 1. Leverage capital do not automatically match you with any user, we give you the freedom to choose a user to pay to from the receiving list that is located at the Make Donation page
  2. 2. At the donation page type in the amount you are willing to donate either in the Naira box or Bitcoin box (if you wish to donate in bitcoin, you will also receive payment in bitcoin)
  3. 3. Select any user from the receivers list and click on reserve.
  4. 4. After reserving a name, you have to make payment within 6hours to avoid getting blocked from the system
After making payment, wait for 7days(3days if its your first time) for your money to mature and on the 7th day your name will enter into the receivers list for people to pay you.
If you refer a friend with your referral code(your referral code is your username), you wil get a bonus of ₦1,000 as soon as your friend makes the first donation. The minimum withdrawal for this bonus is ₦5,000, which means you must accumulate the referral bonus till it gets to ₦5,000 afterwhich you can now withdraw.

In order to make the system sustainable and long lasting, the Leverage capital team has included this recommitment pattern.

After you make your first deposit and your money is matured, before you make the withdrawal, you will need to make another donation that is equal or greater than the initial amount you paid.

After making this recommitment you can now withdraw, and the donation you recommited will immediately start growing again

This recommitment pattern ensures that there is always money in the system and this will make the system long lasting.
